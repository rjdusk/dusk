introduction: <p>Working as the in-house creative for this commercial enzyme company in Tromsø, Norway has been a real delight and an eye opener. My responsibilities included graphic design, front-end development, photography, illustration, plus much, much more. ArcticZymes leverage their access to the marine Arctic to identify novel cold-adapted enzymes for use in molecular research, diagnostics, and manufacturing.</p>
