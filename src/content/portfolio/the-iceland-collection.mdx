description: A road trip over the course of 2 weeks following the “ring road” was pure bliss and has left me with a longing to explore Iceland more.
introduction: <p>Over the course of 2 weeks during the Icelandic “summer” of 2011, my wife and I headed to Iceland with some good friends from Germany. We sketched out a rough plan on the flight from Oslo to Reykjavik, rented an SUV and followed the ring round that circles Iceland.</p><p>The drive from the airport to Reykjavik was mind blowing in itself and really got me pumped for what else we might find out there. The ensuing 12 days was a feast of epic landscapes, jawdropping waterfalls, majestic puffins and dust storms that taught me a very tough lesson in keeping camera equipment protected.</p><p>If you're a photographer, this country must be visited and explored by foot to get a real appreciation for it's magnificence.</p>
